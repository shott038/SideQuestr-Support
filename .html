SideQuestr Support How Can We Help? Having trouble with the app? We're here to help. Common Questions How do I record a visit? Tap "Record Visit" on the map screen when you're near a spot. You must be within range of the location to check in. How do I upload a new spot? Tap "Upload Spot" on the map screen. Add a name, category, photo, and drop a pin on the location. How does XP work? You earn XP by visiting spots and exploring new areas. Track your progress on your profile. How do thumbs up/down work? Each spot has community ratings. Thumb up spots you enjoyed, thumb down ones that weren't worth the trip. This helps other explorers find the best places. My location isn't working. Make sure you've granted SideQuestr location permissions in Settings → SideQuestr → Location → "While Using the App." I want to delete my account. Send us an email at the address below and we'll process your request within 30 days. Report a Problem If you've found a bug or something isn't working right, email us with: - What you were trying to do - What happened instead - Your device model and iOS version Contact Email: sidequestr.support@gmail.com We typically respond within 48 hours.
